How a build happens

From consultation to delivery.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We talk through use-case, budget and any parts you already own.

  2. 02

    Configuration

    Parts are selected and checked for compatibility, with alternatives if something's out of stock.

  3. 03

    Assembly & Testing

    Built on the bench, stress-tested, and cable-managed before it ever reaches the case's final close-up.

  4. 04

    Handover

    Walkthrough of the build, warranty terms, and what to do if anything needs attention.
